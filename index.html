Welcome to Stats Education!



This website is the creation of Adam J Sullivan, PhD. This site will provide basic statistics education for free. Currently we have an Introduction to R course and will be adding a lot more courses in the future.


All of the education material here is free and the documents creating all of it are available through Github. All of the education Materials are also created or compiled by Brown University Department of Biostatistics Professors. Some of these materials are used in our courses as well.

About the Creator

Adam J Sullivan is an Assistant Professor of Biostatistics at Brown University. His interests include flipped/blended learning, online learning, open education, R programming and Statistics Education. He is currently director of the Masters program in biostatistics at Brown University and the faculty statistician at the Hassenfeld Child Health Innovation Institute.
